Why is it called Waving Cat Consultants?

The waving cat is known to the Chinese as “Jinmao” and it beckons good fortune to its owners. Many years ago the founder brought a waving cat in China Town and kept it in his office. It became a personal trade mark and the success in winning projects and funding seemed to get better. Use Waving Cat Consultants for your projects and you too can share in the good fortune.
