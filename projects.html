Current and Recent Projects

  • Developing a public sector property strategy for the West London Alliance to show how assets could be used to increase the supply of affordable housing, improve public services and create a financial return. This involved developing data base of public sector land and building in West London covering all public bodies including local government, the NHS, GLA family and central government. More than 2000 properties have been identified. 4 exemplar sites were developed to show how existing public buildings could be intensified. We showed on these 4 sites they could generate new buildings for public services, 300 new homes and bring in £20m of new capital funding.

  • Developing major new revenue streams for the public sector by developing sites for digital outdoor adverts. This has included site identification, negotiating a profit share agreement with London Underground and managing sensitive sites through to planning permission.

  • Developing new approaches to the problems of young people who go long term into the NEETS group under the government’s whole place budgeting project.

  • Managing site and asset disposal –This included options appraisals through the management of the actual disposal and contract negotiation. Sites have included sensitive sites including a listed public library and former day centre which was sold to Pocket Living.

  • Creation of new civic public space as a focus for a new area development project at North Acton. This has included managing CPO process, site purchase from a partner, contractor selection, overseeing design development and obtaining planning permission.

  • Developing the strategy and programme for the redevelopment of a main council office site to create new offices and homes. This will be a £200m+ project.
